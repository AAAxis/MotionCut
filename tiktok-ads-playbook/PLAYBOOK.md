## STEP 1: Business Profile & Strategic Context

### CreatorAI Product Summary

Based on codebase analysis, CreatorAI is a native iOS/Android video editor with these core features:

- **AI Reel Generation**: Describe a concept, get a viral POV reel in seconds (Seedance Lite, Seedance Pro, Kling v2.1 engines)
- **AI Ad Creator**: Paste a product URL + creative direction, get a video ad (full pipeline for 10 credits)
- **Video Editor**: Trim, subtitles, music, compression, clips timeline
- **Avatar Picker**: AI avatar selection for generated content
- **Credit-Based Monetization**: RevenueCat-powered IAPs for credit packs (5-15 credits per video depending on engine)
- **Multi-language support**: 21+ localized languages

### Business Type Classification

CreatorAI is a **Mobile App (Freemium/Credits)** product. Here is how this maps against archetypes:

| Dimension | Mobile App (CreatorAI) | Ecommerce DTC | Local Service |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary Objective** | App Installs -> In-App Purchase | Website Conversions / Purchase | Lead Generation |
| **Funnel Structure** | Install -> Onboard -> First Generate -> Buy Credits | View -> ATC -> Purchase | Click -> Form -> Booking |
| **Best Creative Style** | Screen recordings, UGC demos, before/after transformations | Product-in-use UGC, unboxing | Testimonial, results showcase |
| **Key Metrics** | CPI, Day 1 Retention, Cost per First Generation, LTV:CAC | ROAS, CPA, AOV | CPL, Cost per Booking, Show Rate |
| **Starter Budget** | $50-100/day | $50-150/day | $30-75/day |
| **Scaling Budget** | $300-1,000+/day | $500-5,000+/day | $150-500/day |

**For the rest of this playbook, all outputs are optimized for CreatorAI as a mobile app install campaign.**


**Budget Strategy**:
- **ABO (Ad-group Budget Optimization)** for testing phase - gives each audience equal learning opportunity
- $50/day per ad group = $150/day total testing budget
- Minimum 7-day run before making kill/scale decisions

**Decision Criteria**:
| Metric | Kill Threshold | Watch Zone | Scale Trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| CPI | > $5.00 after 3 days | $3.00-5.00 | < $2.00 consistently |
| CTR | < 0.5% after 1,000 impressions | 0.5-1.0% | > 1.5% |
| CVR (click to install) | < 5% | 5-15% | > 20% |
| Hook Rate (2-sec view %) | < 25% | 25-40% | > 50% |
| Completion Rate | < 5% | 5-15% | > 20% |
| Spend vs. results | $100+ with 0 installs | On track | Profitable |

**Kill/Scale Rules**:
1. **Kill an ad**: If CPI > 2x target after $30 spend (approx 15x target CPI)
2. **Kill an ad group**: If no ad group achieves < $4 CPI after 5 days
3. **Scale an ad**: If CPI < $2 and CTR > 1% for 3 consecutive days
4. **Scale an ad group**: Increase budget by 20-30% every 48 hours (avoid >30% jumps to preserve learning)

#### Scaling Phase (Week 4+)

**Horizontal Scaling** (audience expansion):
1. Duplicate winning ad groups with new targeting:
   - Broad targeting (let algorithm find users)
   - Lookalike audiences based on installers
   - Lookalike audiences based on purchasers (credit buyers)
   - New interest combinations
   - New geos (Tier 2 countries)

2. Creative expansion:
   - Take winning hooks and pair with new bodies
   - Take winning concepts and reshoot with different creators
   - Test new formats (green screen, duet-style, etc.)

**Vertical Scaling** (budget increases):
- Increase winning ad group budgets by 20% every 2-3 days
- Switch to CBO once you have 2-3 proven ad groups
- CBO daily budget = sum of individual ad group budgets + 20%

**Creative Refresh Cadence**:
- Add 2-3 new ads per ad group every 7-10 days
- Pause ads with declining CTR (>30% drop from peak over 3 days)
- Maintain 4-6 active ads per ad group at all times
- Full creative refresh every 3-4 weeks (new scripts, new creators, new angles)

### 5D: Targeting Strategy

**Testing Phase Targeting**:

| Ad Group | Targeting Type | Details | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Broad | No targeting | Age 18-44, All genders | Let TikTok's algorithm find the best users. Often outperforms interest targeting. Requires creative to do the targeting. |
| Interest - Creators | Interest-based | Interests: Video Editing, Photography, Content Creation, Social Media Marketing. Behaviors: Creator account, Video upload 5+/month | Reach active creators who already have the editing need |
| Interest - Small Biz | Interest-based | Interests: Ecommerce, Small Business, Entrepreneurship, Digital Marketing. Behaviors: Business account | Reach the AI Ad Creator target segment |

**Scaling Phase Targeting**:

| Type | Details | When to Deploy |
|---|---|---|
| Lookalike - Installers | 1%, 3%, 5% lookalike from installer list | After 1,000+ installs |
| Lookalike - Purchasers | 1%, 3% lookalike from credit buyers | After 200+ purchasers |
| Custom - Engaged | 75%+ video viewers from past 30 days | Retargeting campaign |
| Custom - Lapsed | Installed but not opened in 14+ days | Re-engagement campaign |
| Broad expansion | Remove all targeting, age 18-55 | When broad consistently wins in testing |

**Exclusion Strategy**:
- Exclude existing app installers from all acquisition campaigns (use MMP audience sync)
- Exclude purchasers from install campaigns (move to upsell campaigns)
- Exclude users who clicked but didn't install for 3 days (ad fatigue signal), then re-include in retarget

### 6C: Agent Command Pipeline

**Command 1: `research`**
- **Inputs**: business-profile.yaml, search queries from Step 2
- **Processing**: Execute web searches, visit TikTok Creative Center, catalog competitor ads, log sources
- **Outputs**: research/competitor-log.md, research/source-tracker.md, research/trend-analysis.md
- **Validation**: Minimum 10 sources logged, minimum 8 competitor entries, all sources dated

**Command 2: `synthesize`**
- **Inputs**: research/*.md files
- **Processing**: Identify top 5 hook patterns, top 3 visual formats, top 3 CTAs from competitor data. Cross-reference with business-profile.yaml to find adaptation opportunities.
- **Outputs**: Updated competitor-log.md with analysis column, pattern-summary section in trend-analysis.md
- **Validation**: Each pattern must have 2+ source examples. Each adaptation must be differentiated from source.

**Command 3: `generate-scripts`**
- **Inputs**: trend-analysis.md, business-profile.yaml, compliance-rules.yaml
- **Processing**: Generate 12 concepts (3 hooks each), 3 full scripts (shot-by-shot), 3 variations per script. Run compliance check against compliance-rules.yaml for each script.
- **Outputs**: creatives/concepts/concept-[1-12].md, creatives/scripts/script-[1-3].md, creatives/variations/variation-[1-9].md
- **Validation**: All scripts pass compliance check. All hooks are under 3 seconds. All scripts are 15-35 seconds. Natural spoken language (no jargon).

**Command 4: `output-campaign`**
- **Inputs**: campaign-config.yaml, business-profile.yaml, creatives/scripts/*.md
- **Processing**: Generate campaign structure, targeting strategy, A/B test matrix, pixel checklist, naming convention examples.
- **Outputs**: campaigns/structure.md, campaigns/targeting.md, campaigns/ab-tests.md, campaigns/pixel-checklist.md
- **Validation**: All naming conventions follow schema. All budgets sum to daily ceiling. All ad groups have 3-6 ads.

**Command 5: `compile-playbook`**
- **Inputs**: All files from research/, creatives/, campaigns/, config/
- **Processing**: Assemble into single comprehensive Markdown playbook with table of contents, cross-references, and next-actions checklist.
- **Outputs**: outputs/PLAYBOOK.md (this file), outputs/campaign-settings.json
- **Validation**: All sections present. All tables render correctly. All links resolve. Next-actions checklist has 8-12 items.

### 6D: Guardrails

- **NEVER** request, store, or handle API credentials, passwords, tokens, or MMP keys
- **NEVER** generate content violating TikTok Advertising Policies (see compliance-rules.yaml)
- **NEVER** scrape private user data, DMs, or non-public profile information
- **NEVER** generate content with unsubstantiated health, income, or results claims
- **NEVER** copy competitor creative assets, music, or scripts verbatim
- **ALWAYS** include compliance notes when content borders restricted categories
- **ALWAYS** recommend user verification of all claims and permissions
- **ALWAYS** note when content requires disclaimers (testimonials, before/after, results)
- **ALWAYS** use commercially licensed music only in ad creative recommendations
- **ALWAYS** respect intellectual property -- adapt patterns and principles, never copy assets
