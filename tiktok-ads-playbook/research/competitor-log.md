## Hook Types Reference
- **Pattern Interrupt**: Visual or audio that breaks scrolling pattern
- **Controversial Take**: Bold statement that triggers debate
- **Before/After**: Transformation reveal
- **Problem-Agitate**: Pain point identification and amplification
- **Social Proof**: Results, numbers, testimonials
- **Tutorial/Demo**: "How to" or "Watch me" format
- **Story-Based**: Narrative arc with personal experience
- **Trend-Jack**: Leveraging trending sound, format, or meme
