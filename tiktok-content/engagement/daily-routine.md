# Daily Engagement Routine

## Before Posting (10 min)

- [ ] Scroll FYP — like and comment on 10 posts in creator/tech niche
- [ ] Reply to ALL comments on your most recent post (comment velocity signal)
- [ ] Check DMs — respond to genuine messages
- [ ] Save 1-2 trending sounds for upcoming content

## After Posting (15 min per post)

- [ ] Reply to the first 10 comments within 15 minutes (critical for algorithm)
- [ ] Pin the best comment OR post a question as first comment ("What should I prompt next?")
- [ ] Share to Instagram Stories with "new TikTok!" sticker
- [ ] Post link in any relevant Discord/community channels

## Evening Wrap (10 min)

- [ ] Reply to any remaining comments from the day
- [ ] Follow 5-10 new accounts that engaged with your content
- [ ] Comment on 5 posts from accounts in your target audience

## Comment Templates

### On Your Own Posts (First Comment / Pin)

**Question hooks (pick one per post):**
- "What should I prompt next? Best suggestion gets a video 👇"
- "Which AI engine should I try next — Seedance or Kling?"
- "What's the craziest prompt you can think of? I'll try the top one"
- "Save this for later 🔖 which tip was your favorite?"
- "Who else needs this? Tag them 👇"

### Replying to Comments on Your Posts

**Positive comments:**
- "glad you liked it! wait till you see tomorrow's 🔥"
- "right?? the AI keeps surprising me too"
- "tysm! lmk if you try it — I wanna see your results"

**Skeptical comments:**
- "I was skeptical too until I actually tried it — it's free to download if you wanna see"
- "fair! it's not perfect for everything but for quick reels it's actually wild"
- "that's a valid point — I'm gonna test that and make a follow-up"

**Feature requests:**
- "actually noted — passing this to the team 📝"
- "this is a good idea! might be coming sooner than you think 👀"

**"How" questions:**
- "I'll make a full tutorial on that tomorrow! follow so you don't miss it"
- "the app is called CreatorAI — link in bio! it's free to start"

### Commenting on Other Creators' Posts (Engagement Outreach)

**Rules:**
- Be genuine — add value, don't promote
- Never spam "check out my app" on other people's posts
- Comment something insightful or funny related to THEIR content
- Your profile does the selling (they'll click if curious)

**Templates:**
- "this is exactly why AI editing tools are the future — well said"
- "the editing on this is so clean 🔥 how long did this take you?"
- "I literally just went through this same struggle yesterday 😂"
- "this tip alone saved me hours — bookmarking this"

## Weekly Engagement Rituals

### Sunday Review (20 min)
- [ ] Review weekly analytics (see analytics/kpi-framework.md)
- [ ] Identify top 3 performers — what made them work?
- [ ] Identify bottom 3 performers — what to avoid?
- [ ] Update pinned videos if a new one outperformed
- [ ] Plan next week's flex slots based on trends observed

### Duet/Stitch Session (1x/week, 15 min)
- Find 1 viral video in creator niche
- Stitch or Duet with CreatorAI demo as your addition
- This piggybacks on existing reach — great for growth

### Community Building (1x/week)
- Go live for 15-30 min (even low-follower lives boost account authority)
- Topic: "Let's try your prompts in CreatorAI live"
- Interact with viewers in real-time — creates loyal fans
