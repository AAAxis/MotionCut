# Growth Tactics — CreatorAI TikTok

## Tactic 1: Reply with Video
**Effort:** Medium | **Impact:** High

When someone leaves an interesting comment, film a video response:
- TikTok → Your video → Comments → Tap comment → "Reply with video"
- The response video shows the original comment as a sticker
- Gets shown to the original commenter's followers = new audience
- Best for: "can you try [prompt]?" comments → film the AI making it

## Tactic 2: Stitch Viral Content
**Effort:** Medium | **Impact:** High

Find viral videos about content creation pain points and Stitch them:
- First 3-5 seconds: their viral clip (the problem)
- Remaining 10-15 seconds: your CreatorAI solution
- Inherits some of the original video's distribution
- Best for: videos complaining about editing taking too long

## Tactic 3: Series/Numbered Content
**Effort:** Low per post | **Impact:** Medium (compounds)

Running series (Day 1 of..., Hack #1, AI Makes #1) create:
- Follow incentive (people want to see the next one)
- Serial viewers boost average watch time across your account
- Numbered format creates completionist urge
- Best for: Creator Hack of the Day, AI Makes... series

## Tactic 4: Intentional Controversy
**Effort:** Low | **Impact:** High (but risky)

Mild hot takes that provoke comments:
- "CapCut is overrated. Here's what I use instead." (comparison, not attack)
- "Video editors are going to be replaced by AI" (debate bait)
- "You don't need editing skills to be a content creator anymore"
- Comments = algorithm fuel — both agreements and disagreements help
- RULE: Never be mean. Controversial ≠ offensive. Stick to industry opinions.

## Tactic 5: Intentional "Mistake"
**Effort:** Zero | **Impact:** Medium

Subtly mispronounce a word, misspell something in text overlay, or state a slightly wrong fact:
- Comments flood in to "correct" you
- Comment velocity boosts the video's reach
- Works especially well on educational/tips content
- Don't overuse — once every 2 weeks max

## Tactic 6: Trend Jacking (Speed Is Key)
**Effort:** Medium | **Impact:** Very High (if early)

Being in the first 10% of creators to use a trending sound = 3-5x reach multiplier.
- Check trending sounds daily (see trend-research.md)
- Film and post within 24 hours of spotting a trend
- Even a simple version posted fast beats a polished version posted late

## Tactic 7: Cross-Pollination
**Effort:** Low | **Impact:** Medium

After posting on TikTok:
- Share to Instagram Stories ("New TikTok!" sticker)
- Post to YouTube Shorts (remove TikTok watermark first — use SnapTik or download original)
- Share in relevant Discord/Reddit communities
- Tweet the TikTok link
- Each external source of traffic signals quality to TikTok's algorithm

## Tactic 8: Comment Farming on Large Accounts
**Effort:** Low (5 min/day) | **Impact:** Medium (slow build)

Leave thoughtful, funny, or insightful comments on viral videos in your niche:
- If your comment gets likes/replies, hundreds of people see your profile
- Your profile pic (CreatorAI icon) and username get organic impressions
- NEVER promote in comments — just be genuinely interesting
- Best on videos from accounts with 100K+ followers

## Tactic 9: Collab Videos
**Effort:** High | **Impact:** Very High

Partner with other creators to make content together:
- "I challenged [creator] to make a reel using only AI"
- "We swapped phones and used each other's editing apps"
- Both creators post → cross-audience exposure
- Start with creators of similar size, work up

## Tactic 10: Go Live
**Effort:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium (long-term)

Going live, even to 5 viewers, boosts your account's authority signal:
- "Let's try your prompts in CreatorAI — drop them in chat"
- Live viewers become loyal followers
- TikTok notifies your followers when you go live = re-engagement
- Recommended: 1x/week, 15-30 minutes

---

## Growth Milestones & Strategy Shifts

| Milestone | Focus | Primary Tactic |
|-----------|-------|---------------|
| 0-1K followers | Consistency + trends | Trend jacking + comment farming |
| 1K-10K followers | Series + community | Numbered series + reply with video |
| 10K-50K followers | Collabs + authority | Creator partnerships + masterclass content |
| 50K-100K followers | Virality + brand | High-production wow content + live events |
| 100K+ followers | Monetization + scale | Sponsored content + team content creation |
