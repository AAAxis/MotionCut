# TikTok Algorithm Strategy for CreatorAI

## Algorithm Signal Priority (Ranked by Weight)

### 1. COMPLETION RATE (Highest Weight)
Videos watched to the end rank highest in the recommendation system.

**Our strategy:**
- Keep 60% of posts under 20 seconds — short enough to loop
- Front-load the value: show the AI result in the first 5 seconds, then explain
- Use open loops: "wait for it..." before the AI generation reveal
- Zero dead spots — every second must earn the next second

### 2. REWATCH RATE
Videos watched multiple times signal exceptional quality.

**Our strategy:**
- Fast-paced screen recordings that show details viewers miss first time
- AI generation reveals that are visually complex (viewers rewatch to process)
- "Did you catch that?" — subtle details in the generated video
- Satisfying before/after comparisons viewers want to see again

### 3. SHARE RATE
Shares to DMs and other platforms = strongest distribution signal.

**Our strategy:**
- Create "you NEED to try this" moments — AI turning ridiculous prompts into real videos
- Make content useful enough that creators share with their creator friends
- "Tag a creator who needs this" — social sharing prompts
- Focus on wow factor — the more unbelievable the AI output, the more shares

### 4. COMMENT VELOCITY
Fast comments after posting boost initial reach significantly.

**Our strategy:**
- Ask questions in every video: "What should I prompt next?"
- Use mild controversy: "This will replace video editors" (provokes reactions)
- Intentional "mistakes" — mispronounce something, comments correct you
- Pin a question as first comment to seed discussion

### 5. SAVE RATE
Saves indicate reference-worthy content people want to revisit.

**Our strategy:**
- Tutorial content: "How to add AI subtitles in 3 taps" — people save tutorials
- Tip compilations: "5 AI video hacks you didn't know"
- Step-by-step guides that viewers bookmark for later

### 6. FOLLOW-THROUGH RATE
Viewers who visit profile after watching = authority signal.

**Our strategy:**
- End videos with curiosity: "I'm posting a new AI experiment every day — follow"
- Series content that incentivizes following: "Day X of..."
- Strong profile with pinned best content = converts visitors to followers

## CreatorAI-Specific Algorithm Plays

### The "AI Generation Reveal" Pattern
1. Show the text prompt (simple, relatable)
2. Tap generate → brief suspense
3. REVEAL the AI-generated video → jaw-drop moment
4. This drives: completion (viewers wait for reveal), shares (wow factor), rewatches (visual complexity)

### The "Speed Comparison" Pattern
1. Split screen: manual editing (time-lapse, painful) vs. CreatorAI (10 seconds, effortless)
2. This drives: shares (relatable pain), saves (utility), comments ("no way")

### The "Absurd Prompt" Pattern
1. Type the most ridiculous/creative prompt possible
2. Show AI generation result
3. This drives: comments ("try X next!"), shares (entertainment), rewatches (surprise)

## Algorithm Do's and Don'ts

### DO
- Post at consistent times (algorithm rewards reliability)
- Reply to comments within 15 minutes of posting
- Use trending sounds when they naturally fit
- Create content that works with sound OFF (text overlays)
- Pin top 3 performing videos to profile
- Go live occasionally (boosts overall account authority)

### DON'T
- Don't delete underperforming videos (signals instability to algorithm)
- Don't post more than 3x/day (content competes with itself)
- Don't use the same hashtags on every post (looks spammy)
- Don't buy followers/engagement (algorithm detects and penalizes)
- Don't cross-post with watermarks from other platforms
- Don't use sounds from the personal library for brand content (use Commercial Music Library)
