# Trend Research Framework — Daily Scouting Routine

## Daily Routine (15 minutes)

### Step 1: Scroll FYP (5 min)
- Open TikTok, scroll For You Page
- Screenshot any trending sounds or formats in creator/tech niche
- Note: sound name, format structure, approximate view counts
- Save sounds directly in TikTok (bookmark icon → add to favorites)

### Step 2: Check Trending Sounds (3 min)
- TikTok → Create → Add Sound → Trending
- Filter by "New to TikTok" for early adoption advantage (posting early = 3-5x more reach)
- Save 2-3 sounds that could work with CreatorAI content

### Step 3: Check Competitor Accounts (3 min)
- Visit these accounts daily (top creator tool brands on TikTok):
  - @capcut (benchmark for AI editing content)
  - @canva (design tool social strategy)
  - Any emerging AI video tool accounts
- Sort by "Latest" — what are they posting RIGHT NOW?
- Note any new format or topic you haven't tried

### Step 4: Check TikTok Creative Center (2 min)
- Go to ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter
- Top Ads → filter by "App" category
- Note hooks, formats, and pacing from top-performing paid ads (informs organic)

### Step 5: Hashtag Scout (2 min)
- Search #AIVideo, #VideoEditing, #ContentCreator on TikTok
- Sort by "This week"
- Identify any emerging subtopics or angles with high engagement

## Trend Response Matrix

| Trend Type | Shelf Life | Response Time | Priority |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|----------|
| Trending Sound (viral audio) | 3-7 days | Film within 24 hours | HIGHEST — film same day if possible |
| Format Trend (POV, challenge) | 1-3 weeks | Film within 3 days | HIGH |
| Topic Trend (news, discourse) | 1-2 weeks | Post within 2 days | HIGH |
| Evergreen Format (tutorial, demo) | Months+ | Anytime | MEDIUM — use to fill calendar |
| Seasonal (holiday, event) | Predictable | Plan 1 week ahead | MEDIUM |

## Trend Adaptation Workflow

When you spot a trend:

1. **Screenshot it** — save the original video
2. **Identify the structure** — what's the formula? (hook → setup → punchline?)
3. **Map to CreatorAI** — how does our app fit this format?
4. **Write the script** — adapt the structure, keep the sound, swap in our content
5. **Film within 24 hours** — speed is everything with trends
6. **Tag as Trend Ride** in the calendar

### Trend Adaptation Examples

**Trend:** "Show me X without telling me X" sound
**Adaptation:** "Show me you use AI to edit without telling me" → screen recording of typing prompt, AI generating, result playing

**Trend:** Dramatic reveal sound (classical music builds)
**Adaptation:** Slowly type a mundane prompt → dramatic pause → AI generates stunning result as music drops

**Trend:** "Things that just make sense" voiceover
**Adaptation:** "Things that just make sense: using AI instead of spending 4 hours editing one reel" → show CreatorAI demo

## Red Flags — Trends to SKIP

- Trends involving dangerous activities or challenges
- Trends that require disparaging specific people or brands
- Trends that are culturally sensitive or could be misinterpreted
- Trends already past peak (if every brand is using it, you're too late)
- Trends with copyrighted music not in TikTok's Commercial Music Library
- Trends that don't naturally connect to video editing or content creation
