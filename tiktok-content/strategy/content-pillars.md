# Content Pillars — CreatorAI TikTok

## Pillar Overview

| Pillar | % | TikTok Format | Algorithm Target | Color Code |
|--------|---|--------------|-----------------|------------|
| **Wow Factor** | 25% | AI demo, before/after, screen recording | Shares, rewatches | Purple |
| **Quick Tips** | 25% | Tutorial, hack, how-to | Saves, completion | Blue |
| **Trend Rides** | 20% | Trending sounds/formats + app demo | Completion, reach | Green |
| **Relatable/Funny** | 15% | POV, skit, meme | Comments, shares | Yellow |
| **Behind the Scenes** | 10% | Building in public, team, process | Comments, follows | Orange |
| **Direct Promo** | 5% | Feature launch, update, offer | Profile visits | Red |

## PILLAR 1: WOW FACTOR (25%)

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll with visually stunning AI generation results.
**Algorithm play:** Shares + rewatches — people send these to friends saying "look at this"

### 10 Post Ideas

1. "I typed 5 words and AI made THIS video" — type "sunset beach vibes chill" → show stunning result
2. "AI turned my dog's name into a cinematic trailer" — absurd prompt → surprisingly good output
3. "Seedance Pro vs Seedance Lite — same prompt, different results" — side-by-side comparison
4. "I gave AI the weirdest prompt I could think of" — "underwater pizza delivery on Mars" → result
5. "This AI video took 10 seconds. The manual version took 4 hours." — time comparison
6. "Watch AI create a video ad from just a website URL" — Ad Creator demo with real URL
7. "I let AI pick my avatar and make a reel about my morning routine" — avatar + generation
8. "Kling v2.1 just dropped and the quality is INSANE" — premium engine showcase
9. "Can AI make a better reel than me? Let's find out" — human vs AI challenge
10. "I prompted CreatorAI with every genre and here's what happened" — compilation of 5-6 results

### Formats
- Screen recording + face cam reaction (picture-in-picture)
- Before/after split screen
- "Gallery scroll" of multiple AI outputs
- Single unbroken screen recording from prompt to result

### Caption Template
"[description of what happened] 🤯 the AI actually did THAT

what should I prompt next? drop it below 👇

#CreatorAI #AIVideo #[niche hashtag] #[trending hashtag]"

## Pillar Rotation Rules

- Never post the same pillar twice in a row
- Weekly target: 3-4 Wow Factor, 3-4 Quick Tips, 2-3 Trend Rides, 2 Relatable, 1 BTS, 0-1 Promo
- If a pillar underperforms for 2 consecutive weeks, swap 50% of its slots for the top performer
- Trend Rides are flex — if no good trend available, substitute Wow Factor or Quick Tips
- Direct Promo only when there's genuine news (feature launch, milestone, update)
