# Recurring Content Series — CreatorAI TikTok

## Series 1: "AI Makes..." (3x/week)

**Format:** Screen recording + face cam reaction
**Length:** 12-20 seconds
**Pillar:** Wow Factor
**Hook structure:** "Watch AI make a [genre/style] video from scratch"

**Episode ideas:**
- AI Makes... a horror movie trailer
- AI Makes... a cooking show intro
- AI Makes... a real estate listing video
- AI Makes... a gym motivation reel
- AI Makes... a travel vlog
- AI Makes... a product commercial (using Ad Creator)
- AI Makes... a wedding invitation video
- AI Makes... a pet influencer reel
- AI Makes... a music video
- AI Makes... a news broadcast

**Why it works:**
- Infinite content (every genre = new episode)
- Comments suggest future episodes = engagement
- Each episode introduces the app to a different niche audience
- Satisfying reveal moment = high completion rate

**Series hashtag:** #AIMakes

## Series 2: "Creator Hack of the Day" (Daily or 5x/week)

**Format:** Quick tutorial screen recording
**Length:** 10-18 seconds
**Pillar:** Quick Tips
**Hook structure:** "Creator hack #[number]: [hack title]"

**Episode ideas:**
- #1: Add subtitles in 3 taps
- #2: Use a reference video for better AI results
- #3: The prompt formula that always works: [subject] + [style] + [mood]
- #4: How to pick the right AI engine for your video
- #5: Export at the perfect quality for TikTok
- #6: Use the clips timeline to reorder your reel
- #7: Add trending music without copyright issues
- #8: How to write prompts that make cinematic videos
- #9: Batch-create content in 30 minutes
- #10: The avatar trick for personalized AI reels

**Why it works:**
- Numbered series creates completionist urge to follow
- High save rate (people bookmark tips)
- Easy to batch-film (all screen recordings)
- Establishes authority as THE source for video editing tips

**Series hashtag:** #CreatorHackOfTheDay

## Series 3: "What Would AI Do?" (2-3x/week)

**Format:** Challenge/experiment — submit a prompt, see what AI creates
**Length:** 15-25 seconds
**Pillar:** Relatable/Funny + Wow Factor hybrid
**Hook structure:** "I gave AI the prompt: '[absurd prompt]' — here's what happened"

**Episode ideas:**
- "make me famous" — what does AI think fame looks like?
- "Monday morning motivation for people who hate Mondays"
- "a cat explaining quantum physics"
- "if my coffee could talk"
- "the worst first date ever"
- "what my dog thinks I do all day"
- [Viewer-submitted prompts from comments]

**Why it works:**
- Audience participation (comment your prompt = engagement)
- Unpredictable results = entertaining
- Shows the creative range of the AI
- Viral potential when prompts are funny/relatable

**Series hashtag:** #WhatWouldAIDo

---

## Series 4: "1-Minute Masterclass" (1x/week — Wednesdays)

**Format:** Comprehensive tutorial with screen recording + voiceover
**Length:** 45-60 seconds
**Pillar:** Quick Tips (deep version)
**Hook structure:** "Everything you need to know about [topic] in 60 seconds"

**Episode ideas:**
- "Everything about AI video engines — Seedance Lite vs Pro vs Kling"
- "The complete guide to writing AI video prompts"
- "How to create a video ad from scratch with AI"
- "Mastering the CreatorAI video editor in 60 seconds"
- "How to go from zero to viral reel using AI"
- "The avatar system explained — AI models vs your own face"
- "How to repurpose one AI video for 5 platforms"

**Why it works:**
- Highest save rate of any format (comprehensive reference)
- Weekly ritual that followers anticipate
- Establishes deep authority on the topic
- Long enough for meaningful value, short enough for completion

**Series hashtag:** #1MinuteMasterclass

## Series Launch Strategy

1. **Week 1:** Launch "AI Makes..." and "Creator Hack of the Day" — these are easiest to produce
2. **Week 2:** Add "What Would AI Do?" — relies on audience participation, need some followers first
3. **Week 3:** Launch "1-Minute Masterclass" — requires more production but builds authority
4. **Ongoing:** Once a series is launched, maintain consistency. Better to post 1 series reliably than 4 series inconsistently.
