# SCRIPT W1-W2: "Why We Built an AI Video Editor"

**Pillar:** Behind the Scenes
**Format:** Talking head (founder/team member)
**Length:** 30 seconds
**Filming:** Front camera, natural setting (desk, office, coffee shop)
**Energy:** Authentic, conversational, passionate

---

## HOOK (0-4 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Talking head, direct eye contact, relaxed setting
**TEXT OVERLAY:** "Why we built CreatorAI 🛠️"
**AUDIO:** "We were tired of spending hours editing one reel."

## BODY (4-25 seconds)

**Shot 1 (4-10s)**
- VISUAL: Same talking head, maybe slight camera movement (handheld feel)
- TEXT OVERLAY: None (let the voice carry)
- AUDIO: "I'm a content creator. I love making videos. But I HATE the editing process. You know that feeling — you have an amazing idea, but by the time you trim, add subtitles, find music, export — the inspiration is gone."

**Shot 2 (10-17s)**
- VISUAL: Brief cutaway to phone screen showing CreatorAI's Reel Creator interface
- TEXT OVERLAY: "So we built this ⬇️"
- AUDIO: "So we asked: what if you could just describe what you want, and AI makes the video for you? That's what CreatorAI does."

**Shot 3 (17-25s)**
- VISUAL: Quick screen recording montage — type prompt → generate → result plays (3-4 second demo)
- TEXT OVERLAY: "Type → Generate → Done ✨"
- AUDIO: "You type a concept. Choose an AI engine. Hit generate. And you get a reel in seconds — with subtitles, music, everything."

## CTA (25-30 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Back to talking head, slight smile
**TEXT OVERLAY:** "What should we build next? 👇"
**AUDIO:** "We're building this for creators like you. So tell me — what feature would make your life easier? Drop it in the comments."

---

## SOUND
**Type:** Original audio only. No background music. Authenticity > production value for BTS content.

## CAPTION
```
started building this because I was spending more time editing than creating 🛠️

now anyone can make a reel in seconds — just describe what you want

what feature should we build next? 👇

#CreatorAI #BuildInPublic #StartupLife #AIVideo #AppDev
```

## FILMING CHECKLIST
- [ ] Find a natural, well-lit spot (window light is perfect)
- [ ] Phone at eye level on tripod or propped up
- [ ] No ring light — natural look is better for BTS content
- [ ] Memorize the key points, DON'T read from a script (ad-lib is better)
- [ ] Film 3 takes — pick the most natural/passionate one
- [ ] Screen record a quick demo to cut in as B-roll
- [ ] Keep the vibe conversational — imagine talking to a friend
- [ ] Total filming: ~15 min | Editing: ~10 min

## HOOK VARIATIONS
- **Alt A:** "Every creator has this one problem. We tried to fix it."
- **Alt B:** "I was editing a 15-second reel for 3 hours. Then I built an AI to do it."
- **Alt C:** "The story of why CreatorAI exists — and what's coming next"
