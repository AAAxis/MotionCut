# SCRIPT W1-M1: "AI Creates a Reel From 5 Words"

**Pillar:** Wow Factor | **Series:** AI Makes... #1
**Format:** Screen recording + face cam reaction (PiP)
**Length:** 15 seconds
**Filming:** Phone screen recording + front camera cutaway
**Energy:** Excited, genuinely surprised

---

## HOOK (0-3 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Close-up of phone screen. Finger types "sunset beach vibes chill" into the Topic/Concept field on CreatorAI's Reel Creator tab.
**TEXT OVERLAY:** "I typed 5 words..."
**AUDIO:** "Okay so I literally just typed five words—"
**RETENTION TRICK:** Open loop — viewer MUST see what happens next

## BODY (3-12 seconds)

**Shot 1 (3-5s)**
- VISUAL: Screen recording — tap the purple "Generate Reel" button. Show the loading/processing animation.
- TEXT OVERLAY: "and the AI made THIS ⬇️"
- AUDIO: "—hit generate, and look at this—"
- NOTE: Speed up loading animation to 1.5x if it takes more than 2 seconds. Don't lose viewers.

**Shot 2 (5-9s)**
- VISUAL: Screen recording — AI-generated video plays in the preview. Let it play for 4 full seconds so viewers see the quality.
- TEXT OVERLAY: None — let the result speak for itself
- AUDIO: [Brief pause for dramatic effect, then] "WAIT. That's actually insane."
- NOTE: Pick a prompt that generates visually stunning output. Reshoot if the first generation isn't impressive enough.

**Shot 3 (9-12s)**
- VISUAL: Cut to front camera — your genuine reaction. Jaw drop, wide eyes, lean back in surprise.
- TEXT OVERLAY: "5 words → full video 🤯"
- AUDIO: "Five words. That's all it took."

## CTA (12-15 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Back to phone screen briefly showing the CreatorAI app icon/home screen
**TEXT OVERLAY:** "CreatorAI — link in bio 🔗"
**AUDIO:** "Link in bio if you wanna try this"
**NOTE:** Keep CTA casual and fast — don't sound like an ad. One sentence max.

---

## SOUND
**Type:** Original audio (your voiceover)
**Background:** None or very subtle beat (keep volume at 10-15%)
**Why:** Original audio can become a sound others reuse = bonus reach
