## THIS IS A FLEX SLOT

This script is a **template** — fill it in the day before or day-of based on what's trending.

## How to Fill This Slot

1. **Tuesday-Thursday:** Scout trending sounds and formats (see trend-research.md)
2. **Thursday evening:** Pick the best-fitting trend
3. **Friday morning:** Film and post

## Adaptation Framework

### If the trend is a SOUND:
- Use the exact sound from TikTok's library
- Structure: [Sound's natural rhythm] + CreatorAI demo/reveal at the drop
- Example: Dramatic build sound → AI generation reveal at the bass drop

### If the trend is a FORMAT (POV, challenge, etc.):
- Follow the exact format structure
- Swap in CreatorAI content where the original had their topic
- Example: "Things that just make sense" → "Using AI instead of editing for 4 hours"

### If the trend is a TOPIC (discourse, news):
- Add CreatorAI's perspective or demo
- Example: "AI is replacing jobs" discourse → show AI making a video → "it's a tool, not a replacement"

## Rules for Trend Rides
- **Speed > polish** — post within 24 hours of spotting the trend
- **Don't force it** — if the trend doesn't naturally connect to CreatorAI, skip it
- **Keep the original structure** — don't over-customize or it won't feel like the trend
- **Use the exact trending sound** — this is how TikTok groups trend content together
