# SCRIPT W1-T1: "POV: Editing ONE Reel for 4 Hours"

**Pillar:** Relatable/Funny
**Format:** POV skit → AI solution reveal
**Length:** 18 seconds
**Filming:** Front camera (face) + screen recording
**Energy:** Dramatic pain → instant relief → excited

---

## HOOK (0-3 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Front camera — dead inside face. Messy hair optional. Staring at phone with glazed eyes. Dark under-eye circles (exaggerate with makeup if needed).
**TEXT OVERLAY:** "POV: you've been editing ONE reel for 4 hours"
**AUDIO:** [No voiceover — let the face and text do the work]
**SOUND:** Slow, sad/dramatic trending sound (first 3 seconds)
**RETENTION TRICK:** Every creator has lived this. Pure relatability = they can't scroll away.

## BODY (3-13 seconds)

**Shot 1 (3-6s)** — The Pain
- VISUAL: Same face. Slowly zoom in. Maybe a single tear (comedic). A text notification appears: "hey is the reel ready?" — face gets MORE defeated.
- TEXT OVERLAY: "3 hours of trimming. Still not right."
- AUDIO: [Still the dramatic sound building]

**Shot 2 (6-8s)** — The Discovery
- VISUAL: Sudden expression change — eyebrows raise. Looking at phone with curiosity. As if they just found something.
- TEXT OVERLAY: "wait... what is this app?"
- AUDIO: [Music shifts — upbeat transition sound or record scratch]

**Shot 3 (8-13s)** — The Solution
- VISUAL: Screen recording — quickly type a prompt in CreatorAI Reel Creator → tap Generate → show the result playing. Speed this up to feel fast and effortless.
- TEXT OVERLAY: "10 seconds later: ✅"
- AUDIO: [Upbeat sound continues — triumphant/relieved]

## CTA (13-18 seconds)

**VISUAL:** Cut back to face — now looking smug/relieved/happy. Hair fixed. Confidence restored.
**TEXT OVERLAY:** "CreatorAI 🔗 link in bio"
**AUDIO:** [Sound fades out]
**FINAL TEXT:** "the app that saved my sanity 😮‍💨"
