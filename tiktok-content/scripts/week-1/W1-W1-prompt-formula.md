- AUDIO: "The formula is: subject, plus style, plus mood, plus vibe."

**Shot 3 (11-16s)** — Good Example
- VISUAL: Screen recording — typing a GOOD prompt: "tropical beach sunset, cinematic drone shot, peaceful, golden hour vibes"
- TEXT OVERLAY: "✅ Good: 'tropical beach sunset, cinematic drone shot, peaceful, golden hour'"
- AUDIO: "...try 'tropical beach sunset, cinematic drone shot, peaceful, golden hour vibes.'"
- Show the stunning result (3s)

**Shot 4 (16-18s)** — Side by Side
- VISUAL: Split screen of bad vs good result
- TEXT OVERLAY: "Same topic. Better prompt. 🔥"
